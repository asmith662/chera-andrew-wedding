How We Met


We met online through a dating app,
and it took a few weeks before we were
brave enough to meet in person.
When it finally happened, we decided
to meet for ice cream and to take a walk
in the local park. I was so neverous
that I started shaking, but she just
kept smiling at me and it encouraged me
on. It didn't take long for us to realize
that we had something, and we have been
inseparable ever since.
